Lessons Learned

1.

AI excels at crisis coordination, not just coding

Triage, prioritization, research, documentation — the stuff you can’t think straight about when you’re stressed

2.

Context is the killer feature

Agent already knew my cards, accounts, and budget. No explaining. It just started working.

3.

Multi-agent systems shine in real emergencies

Treasurer handled finances. Sasha handled work admin. Parallel, not serial.

4.

The human still does the hard parts

Phone calls, identity verification, filing legal documents. AI can’t do those. But it can make sure you don’t miss anything.
