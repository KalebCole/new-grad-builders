Telegram Bot

Give your agent a way to talk to you

1. Create the bot

Open Telegram → search @BotFather → send /newbot → follow prompts → copy the token

2. Add the token to your VM

echo 'your-token' | sudo tee /etc/openclaw/secrets/telegram_token

sudo chmod 600 /etc/openclaw/secrets/telegram_token

3. Talk to your agent

Open the bot chat in Telegram → send a message → your agent responds

Why Telegram? Dedicated window. You don’t want your AI agent in your iMessages.
